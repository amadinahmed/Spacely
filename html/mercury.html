Planet Mercury

Information From : NASA

_______________________________

Mercury is the smallest, ringless and lifeless planet in our solar system. It is estimated to be slightly bigger than Earth’s moon. Mercury is also the closest planet to the Sun with a distance of about 41 million miles, but it’s actually not the hottest. Venus is the hottest planet known in the solar system. Along with Venus, Earth, and Mars, Mercury is one of the “rocky planets”. It has a solid surface that is covered with craters and has a thin atmosphere, and it doesn’t have any moons. If you were to be standing on Mercury, the Sun would appear 3 times larger and 7 times brighter than how it would if you were on Earth.
