Planet Neptune

Information From : NASA

_______________________________

Neptune is a dark, windy, and lifeless planet. It is the most distant planet in our solar system being more than 30 times as far from the Sun as Earth is. Neptune is very similar to Uranus. It is made of a thick consistency of water, ammonia, and methane over an Earth-sized solid center. Its atmosphere is made of hydrogen, helium, and methane. The methane also gives Neptune the same blue color as Uranus. Neptune has six rings, but are difficult to see.It is estimated to be about 4 times wider than Earth and has 14 total moons, 1 in which are waiting to be confirmed.
