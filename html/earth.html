Planet Earth

Information From : NASA

_______________________________

Being the third planet from the Sun with a distance of about 93 million miles, our home planet, Earth, is known as a rocky, terrestrial planet. It has a solid and active surface with mountains, valleys, canyons, plains, etc. Earth is special because it is the only planet in the solar system that has water on its surface. Water covers about 70 percent of Earth's surface and land covers the remaining proportion. Earth's atmosphere is made mostly of nitrogen and has plenty of oxygen to support living forms. The atmosphere also protects us from incoming meteoroids, causing the rock break up into many smaller pieces before they can hit the surface. Earth consists of one moon and no rings. It is the fifth largest planet in the solar system but the bigger within the 4 planets in the asteroid belt.
