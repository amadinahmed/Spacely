Planet Saturn

Saturn is not the only planet that have rings, but it definitely has the most beautiful ones! The rings are made of groups of tiny ringlets that surrounds Saturn. They are made of chunks of ice and rock. The planet has seven rings with gaps and division between each to separate and allow the rings to be visible. But Saturn may appear ringless twice every 29.5 years which makes the planet more unique. Saturn is the sixth planet from the Sun and is the second largest in the solar system. Like Jupiter, Saturn is mostly a ball of hydrogen and helium. It also does not support living form but some of its moons have condition in which support life. Saturn has 62 total moons, 9 in which are waiting to be confirmed.
