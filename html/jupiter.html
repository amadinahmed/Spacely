Planet Jupiter

Information From : NASA

_______________________________

Jupiter is the biggest planet in the solar system and is covered in swirling cloud stripes. The swirling cloud stripes are actually cold, windy clouds of ammonia and water. It is similar to a star, but it never grew to the appropriate size to start burning. Jupiter has big storms such as the Great Red Spot, which has been going for hundreds of years. It is a gas giant and does not have a solid surface, but it may have a solid inner core about the size of Earth. Jupiter also has very faint rings which may be hard to see clearly and consists of 75 moons. Although Jupiter cannot support living forms, some of its moons have oceans beneath its crusts which might be able to support life.
